SERVING CHILDREN SANDWICHES
Take gluten-free-ingredients and gluten-free-bread and make a-gluten-free-sandwich
Put this-sandwich on a-tray
Move the-tray containing sandwich from the-kitchen to the-table where allergic-child is sitting
Serve the-gluten-free-sandwich to the allergic-child
Move the-tray back from the-table to the-kitchen
Take gluten-ingredients and gluten-bread and make a-sandwich which contains gluten
Put this-sandwich on a-tray
Move the-tray from kitchen to the-table where non-allergic-child is sitting
Serve the-gluten-sandwich to the non-allergic-child
Move the-tray back from the-table to the-kitchen
