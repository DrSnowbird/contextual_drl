Call 911
Close all the doors to the room of fire origin or other hazard when leaving.
Activate the fire alarm at the nearest manual station located in the hallway, if necessary.
Leave the building through the nearest exit.
Do not use the elevator; use the stairwell or proceed to the Area for Evacuation Assistance.
Do not leave any doors open behind you. Unlock and close all doors if possible.
Never open doors that feel hot to the touch, use a different exit.
Never attempt to travel through smoke-filled or other imminently hazardous area.
If you encounter smoke in stairwells, close the door and use a different exit.
After evacuation, move away from the building. Never assemble near exits or fire lanes.
